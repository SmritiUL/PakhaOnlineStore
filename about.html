About Us

Pakha Grocery is a digital platform that enables customers to purchase groceries and household products online from the comfort of their homes. Customers can browse through a wide selection of organic products, add items to their cart, and checkout using a variety of payment methods. Pakha Grocery offers a wide range of delivery options, including same-day delivery, next-day delivery, and scheduled delivery. Here at Pakha Grocery, We work with farmers all across Nepal and help them to sell their products to a broad range of consumers. Pakha Grocery ensure that only the organic and authetic products are available for our consumers as we believe our customer deserve the best products from every part of the country.
